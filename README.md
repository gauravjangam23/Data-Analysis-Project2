# Project: Mobile Sales Analytics Dashboard
## Project Objective
To analyze mobile sales data and develop an interactive dashboard using Microsoft Power BI that provides insights into sales performance, customer behavior, and regional trends to support data-driven business decisions.

## Dataset used 
-	<a href=https://github.com/gauravjangam23/Data-Analysis-Project2/blob/main/Mobile%20Sales%20Data.xlsx>Dataset</a>
## Key Business Questions Answered
-	What is the overall sales performance (Total Sales, Quantity, Transactions, Average Sales)? 
-	Which cities/regions generate the highest revenue? 
-	What are the monthly and daily sales trends? 
-	Which mobile brands and models are top-performing? 
-	How do customer ratings vary across products? 
-	What are the most preferred payment methods (UPI, Card, Cash)? 
-	Which factors influence high sales and customer satisfaction?

## Key Insights
-	Total sales reached 769M, with 19K units sold across ~4K transactions, indicating strong market demand 
-	Major cities like Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore contribute significantly to overall revenue 
-	Sales show monthly fluctuations, with peak performance observed in certain months (e.g., March/July trends) 
-	Top brands/models consistently drive the majority of sales volume 
-	Customer ratings are mostly concentrated around 4–5 stars, indicating overall satisfaction 
-	Digital payment methods (UPI, Cards) dominate over cash transactions 
-	Certain products show a strong correlation between high ratings and high sales, indicating customer preference patterns 

## Final Conclusion
The dashboard demonstrates that sales performance is driven by a combination of regional demand, product popularity, and customer satisfaction,
Using Microsoft Power BI and Power Query, the project transforms raw sales data into meaningful insights through interactive visualizations.

The analysis helps :
-	Identify high-revenue regions and top-performing products 
-	Understand customer behavior and payment preferences 
-	Enable businesses to make data-driven decisions for sales optimization
